Bookworms

Title: Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows

Author: J.K.Rowling

Genre: Action Adventure

Description: The novels follow Harry Potter, an 11-year-old boy who discovers he is the son of famous wizards and will attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Harry learns of an entire society of wizards and witches. He befriends several Hogwarts students and teachers along his journey.
